Why Brass?

Brass instruments are some of the most powerful and expressive instruments in the world of music. From the bright sound of the trumpet to the deep voice of the tuba, brass instruments can create a huge range of emotions and colours.

What are Brass Instruments?

Brass instruments create sound when the player's lips vibrate against the mouthpiece. The vibration travels through the tubing, producing different notes.
